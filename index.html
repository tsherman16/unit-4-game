Suns Point Collector

You are given a random "Target Score" at the start of the game. Your starting five are shown below.

By clicking on a Suns player you will add a specific amount of points to your "Current Score". You win the game by matching your "Current Score" to the random "Target Score" shown. You lose the game if the "Current Score" goes above that random "Target Score".

The value of each player is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the value of each of the players change.
