Points Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four basketball players below. By clicking on a player you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number shown. You lose the game if your total score goes above that random number. The value of each player is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the value of each of the players change.
